Whether I'm designing a logo, writing a PHP script, learning to play a difficult passage of music, editing a music video, or working through calculus problems, I can apply a logical process to the problem that will lead me to the best solution. I have a talent for breaking a complex problem down into it's constituent events, tracing them back to the root causes, identifying the right tools to use, and putting it all together to develop meaningful and impactful solutions.
